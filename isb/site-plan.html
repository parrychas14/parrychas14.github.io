Burglars

Burglars are common threats to any household. They could come at any time, and seize your belongings, in order to make some money themselves. Commonly the best way to take care of a burglar is with locks for every possible entry, as well as electronics such as alarms that can be placed all around the building, and security cameras, that should help when dealing with police on the matter. Overall, this is probably the least serious threat you could face out of any that we know of.
